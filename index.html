Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions online.

Do you make interactive animations for websites and apps?

Yes, I specialize in interactive Rive animations for web and mobile products.

How much do your services cost?

My pricing depends on the scope and complexity of the project, so each project is estimated individually. My hourly rate is $18/h. We can also discuss a fixed price for your project if needed.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary depending on the project size, but I always aim to deliver high-quality work on agreed deadlines. As a general estimate: Character illustration/design usually takes from 1 week. A set of 5 animations starts from around 1.5 weeks and can take longer depending on complexity and scope of work.

Can you handle both design and animation?

Yes, I can take care of the full process — from character illustration and graphic design to interactive animation in Rive. I can work on either part separately or deliver a complete solution depending on your needs and project requirements.

Do you take freelance or long-term projects?

I’m open to both freelance projects and long-term collaborations, depending on the scope and needs.
